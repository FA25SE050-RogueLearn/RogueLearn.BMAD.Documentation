### **UC-04: Study Party Collaboration**

This use case details the creation and management of small, focused study groups for collaborative learning, featuring AI-powered resource organization.

**Actor:** Party Leader (Student A), Party Member (Student B)

---

## Transactions

1.  **Student A creates a Note in their Arsenal**
    *   Student A navigates to their "Arsenal" section.
    *   They click "Create Note" and name it "PRJ301 - Study Guide".
    *   Student A writes down important concepts, formulas, and example problems for PRJ301.
    *   They can also use a browser extension to create notes from web pages, such as lecture slides or online tutorials.
    *   THey can also upload images, files, or links to create more detailed notes.

2.  **Student A Creates a Study Party**
    *   After playing a challenging quest, Student A gets a suggestion: "Create a study party!"
    *   They click the suggestion, name the party "PRJ - HJF - SBA Party", and link the relevant subjects.

3.  **Party Leader Invites Members**
    *   As the Party Leader, Student A invites their friend, Student B, by searching for their username.

4.  **Members Share Learning Materials**
    *   Student B accepts the invitation.
    *   Student A shares the "PRJ301 - Study Guide" note they created in their Arsenal with the party.

5.  **Party Members Collaborate on Study Material**
    *   Student B views the shared "PRJ301 - Study Guide" note.
    *   They can comment on specific sections, ask questions, or suggest improvements.
    *   Student A can also view and respond to these comments.

6.  **Party Leader Schedules a Recurring Meeting**
    *   Student A navigates to the "Schedule" channel and sets up a recurring weekly study session, including a meeting link and the meeting title.

7.  **System Sends Automated Reminders**
    *   The system sends notifications to all members before the scheduled meeting.

8.  **Members Join and Conduct the Study Session**
    *   Members join the meeting link at the scheduled time.
    *   They use RogueLearn's shared digital whiteboard feature to collaboratively work through a problem, referencing the AI-tagged notes from the Stash.
    *   They can all join the google meet session created from the meeting.

9.  **Party Leader Ends the Session and Submits a Summary**
    *   After the meeting, Student A clicks "End Meeting" and writes a brief summary of what was discussed and any action items.
    *   The system saves this summary and sends it to all party members. The party's dashboard is updated to show the meeting was held.
